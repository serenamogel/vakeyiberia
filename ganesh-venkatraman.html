Ganesh Venkatraman is a film and TV actor, born on 20th March 1980, Mumbai, hailing from India. He belongs to a Hindu family and has been working in the Hindi, Telugu, Tamil, cinema, and entertainment media industry. He has worked in many Hindi, Tamil and Telugu movies for which he gained huge acclaim. Moreover, he has also worked in several TV serials (Tamil, Telugu, and Hindi) and earned much recognition for his outstanding work. While he is mainly known for “Antariksh”.

Career

Ganesh Venkatraman took the start of his career in the year 2006 and appeared in the Tamil debut movie named “Abhiyum Naaum” which was directed by Radha Mohan and produced by Prakash Raj Productions. While he made his television debut in the same year with a most popular TV serial named “Antariksh-Ek Amar Katha” for which he became popular among his fans and received much praise from them. In the serial, he portrayed the role of Amar, and fortunately, his fans liked him and his wonderful performance. He has also worked for many notable brands including LLC, Nescafe Sunrise, Close up, Medimix Soap, Gabbana, Pothys, Gillette, Reebok, and other big brands are worth sharing. However, he has appeared in various serials and movies for which he has gained immense love from fans and much praise for his absolutely amazing performances. He entered the field of modeling during his college days and he did various modeling tasks before moving on to the small screen and movies. He also worked in the Tamil TV series named “Mayavi” as it was the first 3D TV show in which he played a significant role. In 2017, he took part in the Reality TV show “Bigg Boss” where he appeared as a contestant and became a third runner-up on the show.

Achievements

In 2009, the most talented man was awarded the Best Debut Award for the Tamil Debut movie “Abhiyum Naaum” from Tamil Nadigar Sangam. While he was also honored with the Edison Award for the category of Best Supporting Actor for his remarkable performance in ‘Unnai Pol Oruvan’ in the year 2010.
