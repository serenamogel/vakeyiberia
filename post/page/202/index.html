Whole-Drive Fill: Testing SLC Cache Size Most modern drives, in order to accelerate writing speed, designate a part of the drive as an 'SLC cache'. This is part of the storage that acts in 'single bit-per-cell' mode, which allows for faster reads and writes. Why my Kenmore washer will not spin?

published on March 24, 2024
Answer If the washer would not spin or agitate, the drive belt should be checked. The washer will not spin or agitate properly if the belt is broken or if it is not tightly wound around the pulleys.
