 Kurt Braunohler Biography

Kurt Braunohler is an American comedian as well as co-host of the podcast Bananas on Exactly Right Podcast Network. He was born on February 22, 1976, in Neptune Township, New Jersey, United States. Kurt was previously the host of IFC’s comedy game show Bunk and has appeared on Comedy Central, This American Life, and Radiolab.

Kurt is a frequent collaborator with Kristen Schaal, with whom he created the web series Penelope Princess of Pets.
