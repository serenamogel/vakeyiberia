Did Nic Kerdiles have kids with Savannah Chrisley?

On January 11, 1994, Kerdiles was born in the United States. As a youngster, he competed in the famous 2007 Quebec International Pee-Wee Hockey Tournament, showcasing his physical skills.

He played for a minor ice hockey club from the beautiful city of Los Angeles during this competition, showcasing his skills as a young, talented player. He had no idea that his early introduction to the world of professional hockey would be the first step in that direction.

Nic Kerdiles children: Did Nic Kerdiles have kids with Savannah Chrisley?

No, Nic Kerdiles did not have any children with Savannah Chrisley. They were engaged in 2019, but they broke up in 2020. Kerdiles died in a motorcycle accident in September 2023, at the age of 29.

Chrisley has said that she is still grieving the loss of Kerdiles and that she is focused on taking care of her brother Grayson and her niece Chloe. She has not spoken publicly about any plans to have children of her own.
