JOEY Graziadei had his first one-on-one date as The Bachelor - and fans are convinced they know how the season ends.

Joey, 28, spent his first one-on-one date with Daisy Kent, 25, who opened up about having a cochlear implant and ended up getting a kiss and a rose.

Fans on social media were quick to show their love for the new couple as they enjoyed a helicopter ride and a music festival together on their first date.

"I’m obsessed with Daisy & Joey," one wrote on X, formerly known as Twitter, as another shared, "I love Daisy & Joey together!!! I loved their on on one. Team Daisy for the win."

Other viewers were convinced that Daisy is Joey's final pick after a commercial for Daisy Sour Cream played during the show.

"The daisy sour cream commercial, it’s a sign daisy wins," one viewer theorized.

"My husband thinks it was no coincidence that the first commercial was for Daisy sour cream! Hmmm…," another shared.

  • Joey was 'blown away' by Kelsey Anderson

    After last week's Season 28 premiere, Joey revealed some of his first impressions of the contestants in a conversation with ABC's On The Red Carpet.

    The leading man confessed that he was "blown away" by Kelsey Anderson, 25, when he saw her come out of the limousine at the iconic Bachelor Mansion.

    "Kelsey A was stunning," Joey said in a clip of the interview.

    "As soon as she walked out of the limo, I knew I was blown away.

    "And she's got a goofy side that I knew right from the first conversation we had that night that there was something there that I wanted to, again, learn more about."

  • Joey is sparking DWTS rumors

    Joey Graziadei is already sparking rumors about his future on Dancing with the Stars after he appeared on Live with Kelly and Mark last week where he was asked if DWTS was in his grand plan.

    When the show came back from commercial, Kelly Ripa welcomed back the viewers.

    "Welcome back. Joey's here, telling us about the Bachelor," she said.

    "Ok. isn't this really, confirm or deny, Joey, isn't it really your grand plan to get on Dancing with the Stars? Yes or no?"

    Joey laughed and said it wasn't his grand plan.

    "If that call comes, I would be open to it, but too be honest, I'm trying to do one reality show at a time right now, so let's get through this one first," he said.

  • What to know about Joey

    Hailing from Royersford, Pennsylvania, Joey Graziadei is a corporate sales professional turned tennis coach known for appearing in The Bachelorette’s 20th season.

    After graduating from West Chester University in media studies and communications, he worked in corporate sales.

    Eventually, he turned his professional interests towards his tennis career and moved to Hawaii.

    Joey describes himself as “an avid star gazer” and a “die-hard fan” of the Green Bay Packers.

    He also considers golf and country music to be obsessions of his.
