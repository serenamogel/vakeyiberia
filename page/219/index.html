Last name SITCH: origin and meaning

published on March 08, 2024
Sitch : 1: English (southern): topographic name from Middle English sich(e) ‘stream ditch’ (Old English sīc) for someone who lived near a small stream. Compare Sykes which is from a northern English word of Scandinavian origin with a similar meaning.3: Americanized form of Czech Síč and Sič (see Sic 1) or Rusyn (from Slovakia) and Serbian Sič (see Sic 3).2: Americanized form of Hungarian Szűcs (see Szucs).
