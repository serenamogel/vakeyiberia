Where is the defrost drain on my Kenmore refrigerator?

Answer

The defrost drain hole is positioned in the middle of the back wall of the freezer, right above the point where the floor slopes up to the back wall. A screw holds a plastic panel in place on the wall. Remove the screw and the panel, and the drain hole will be visible on the other side.
