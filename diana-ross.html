Diana Ross aka Diana Ernestine Earle Ross is an American entertainer and actress. As of 2022, Diana Ross’s net worth is $250 million. She accumulated her net worth through her acting career. She is best known as the lead singer for the group The Supremes. The Supremes was one of the best-selling groups of all time and the most successful in Motown.

The group has recorded 12 singles in the top position of the US Billboard Top 100 Chart. The top singles include Love Child, Baby Love, come see about me, and Where did our love go. She sang in the music genre R&B, pop, soul, Disco, and Dance.

Diana Ross Wiki/Biography

Born on 26 March 1944, Diana Ross’s age is 78 years as of 2022. She was born and raised in a well-settled Christian family in Detroit, Michigan, the United States. She is an American by nationality and has a belief in the Christian religion.

She completed her early schooling at Cass Technical High School, Detroit, Michigan, the United States.

After that, she enrolled herself at a Local College from where she completed her graduation. From the very beginning of her childhood, she was more interested in entertainment and various co-curricular activities rather than in studying.
