Chris Robinson is an American singer, guitarist, and frontman of The Black Crowes. He was born on December 20, 1966 in Marietta, GA and is best known for the group's hit song "Hard to Handle". The Black Crowes have been a popular rock band since the early 1990s and have released several successful albums.

About

American singer, Guitarist, and frontman of The Black Crowes, the group known for hits like "Hard to Handle."

Before Fame

He formed a band with his brother as a teenager and performed in clubs around Atlanta.

Trivia

He has also performed as a solo Artist and released his New Earth Mud album in 2002.

Family Life

He was briefly married to Actress Lala Sloatman. He and actress Kate Hudson later married for seven years, welcoming son Ryder Russell Robinson before divorcing. He married his third wife, Allison Bridges, in 2009. The same year they welcomed a daughter, Cheyenne Genevieve Robinson.

Associated With

His first wife, Lala, is the niece of musician Frank Zappa.
