Scarlet & Violet: Paradox Encounters (Indonesian: Pertemuan Paradoks) is the name given to the special Indonesian-exclusive set released in the Asian Scarlet & Violet Era of the Pokémon Trading Card Game. It is a combination of the Japanese Ancient Roar and Future Flash expansions.

Information

Paradox Encounters is the second Indonesian-exclusive set for the Asian Scarlet & Violet Era of the TCG, released on December 15, 2023. This set combines the Japanese Ancient Roar and Future Flash expansions, and thus it introduces Ancient and Future cards to the Indonesian market for the first time. Each booster pack of Paradox Encounters contains 5 cards.
