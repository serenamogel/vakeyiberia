Don Moen is an American singer, songwriter, pastor, and producer of Christian worship music. He has a non-profit ministry called Worship In Action (WIA) based in Nashville, Tennessee that in partnership with Royal Seed Home (RSH) and through donations are helping privileged lives of 180+ orphans in Ghana.
