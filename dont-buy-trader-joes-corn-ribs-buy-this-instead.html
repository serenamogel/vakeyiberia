Don't Buy Trader Joes Corn Ribs, Buy This Instead!

A few months ago, we tried the viral Trader Joe’s frozen corn ribs. Remember the corn ribs? People were showing you how to make them in the airfryer all over TikTok. Well, we weren’t the biggest fans. But that doesn’t mean you should give up on buying your corn products from Trader Joe’s. Here are our thoughts on the Trader Joe’s corn ribs and the other frozen corn we think you should be buying from TJ’s instead.

DON’T BUY THIS: Trader Joe’s Seasoned Corn Ribs

These weird little pieces of corn feel pointless. We tried them and reviewed them and we just weren’t impressed. They’re super greasy and very salty. But, greasy as they are, somehow if you even slightly overcook them, they become very dry. The shape makes them hard to eat. I’m not sure why you’d buy these other than the novelty factor. Please stop buying the Trader Joe’s frozen corn ribs.

BUY THIS: Trader Joe’s Mexican Style Roasted Corn

If you’re looking to buy frozen corn from TJ’s, this is the route you should be taking. Trader Joe’s corn ribs might be viral, but their frozen, Mexican-style corn actually tastes good. First of all, the corn is cut off the cob making it extremely easy to eat and great for sharing. It comes with a “creamy, smoky sauce that melts off as the corn heats.” Additionally, it’s flavored with lime juice and cilantro. Lastly, it comes with a pack of cotija cheese for topping the dish. This would be great added to a burrito bowl (it might even improve Trader Joe’s frozen chicken burrito bowl) or served alongside some grilled chicken. Once you taste this, you will never consider buying corn ribs again. In fact, you’ll forget corn ribs even exist!
