About

Original cast member of MadTV who achieved fame for her impersonations of Britney Spears and Drew Barrymore. She later played a major role in the sitcom The King of Queens and starred in her own Lifetime series Rita Rocks.

Before Fame

She attended Northwestern University where she studied theatre before moving to Los Angeles.

Trivia

She has provided her voice for the animated series Kim Possible and Family Guy as well as Disney's Meet the Robinsons. 

Family Life
