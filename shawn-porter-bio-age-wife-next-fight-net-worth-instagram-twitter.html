Shawn Porter Biography

The Cleveland born boxer, Shawn Porter is an American Professional boxer known for his aggressive pressure fighting style, physical strength, and high work rate. Porter is a 2-time welterweight world champion having held the WBC title since 2018.

He previously held the IBF title from 2013 to 2014. He was ranked the world’s fourth-best active welterweight by The Ring magazine of September 2018. His nickname is “Showtime”, his fighting style orthodox. What’s more, he is ranked as one of best welterweight by the Transnational Boxing Rankings Board and Boxrec, and fifth by The Ring magazine. He started his boxing career in Wicomico Civic Center in Salisbury, Maryland, on October 3nd, 2008
