Romance is Dead Spoilers: What to Expect from the Latest Chapters

Romance is Dead Spoilers: What to Expect from the Latest Chapters

Romance is Dead is a popular webtoon that follows the story of Luce Grazia, a former noblewoman who was falsely accused of cheating on her fiancé, Felix Sanchert, and kicked out of high society.

She becomes a researcher at the National Laboratory, where she meets Sasha Ponchere, a coworker who becomes her closest friend.

However, her life takes another turn when she receives an offer from the Crown Prince to work on a secret project in the capital.

She soon realizes that there are people who want to help her clear her name, as well as those who want to use her for their own agendas.

If you are a fan of this webtoon and want to know what happens next, here are some spoilers for the upcoming chapters.

Be warned, though, that these spoilers are based on the original web novel and may not match exactly with the webtoon adaptation.
