Who Is Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow? Arthur Labinjo-Hughes Mother

Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow, Arthur Labinjo-Hughes mother, was sentenced to prison for eleven years. Find out more about her in the article below.

Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow was convicted of manslaughter. She was found guilty of mercilessly stabbing her partner. For her horrific crime, Olivia has been sentenced to jail for 11 years.

After Olivia was sent to imprisonment, her son, Arthur Labinjo-Hughes, started living with his biological father. This decision turned out to be unfortunate for the child as he became a victim of both mental and physical torture. Moreover, his biological father, Thomas Hughes, was found guilty of manslaughter too.

In this article, you will get an insight into Olivia, her life, and the crime she has committed. Let us learn about her in detail.

Who Is Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow? Arthur Labinjo-Hughes Mother

Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow is Arthur Labinjo-Hughes’s mother. She was convicted of manslaughter on the grounds of diminished responsibility.

After Olivia’s arrest, Arthur started living with his father and his girlfriend Emma Tustin. As he was subjected to torture, Arthur had bruises all over his body; 130 bruises to be exact. He also had various injuries on different parts of his body.

Arthur was not only deprived of food but was also forced to sleep on the floor. He was poisoned with salt and had no energy to fight or confront his abusers. As a matter of fact, more than six spoons worth of salt was found in his body.

Gary Cunningham Murder

Garry Cunningham was Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow’s boyfriend. He was stabbed to death by Olivia. She was said to be in a drink and drug-fuelled rage during the murder of her boyfriend. Additionally, Olivia didn’t show any emotion at the crime scene.

On the other hand, Olivia stabbed Gary 12 times, and he lost his life. Even Olivia’s son, Arthur, witnessed the crime.

Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow Age & Husband

Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow’s age is 28 years old. Her partner, Gary, was only 29 years old at the time of his death.

Moving on to her husband, Olivia and Thomas Hughes are believed to be married formerly. During the time of Arthur’s death, the couple had already parted ways and were living separately.

Where Is Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow Now?

As Olivia Labinjo-Halcrow has been sentenced to prison for 11 years, she is currently in jail.
