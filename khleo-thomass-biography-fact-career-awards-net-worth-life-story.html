Khleo Thomass biography, fact, career, awards, net worth &amp; life story

Khleo Thomas is an American actor, singer, rapper and entertainer who has an estimated net worth of $500 thousand. He was born Khaleed Leon Thomas on January 30, 1989 in Anchorage, Alaska, USA, and moved to Germany. The family returned to the United States when he was four.

Also known as Khleo, he began his professional acting career in the late 1990s, when he appeared in an episode of the Bill Cosby television show Kids Say the Darndest Things. Since then, he has starred in films such as Holes, Friday After Next, Going to the Mat, Walking Tall and Roll Bounce.

In 2006, Thomas had a guest starring role in the Poppin’ Tags episode of CSI: Crime Scene Investigation. Other guest appearances include Family Law, City Guys, ER, The Wayne Brady Show, The Bernie Mac Show, Teachers, House M.D., 90210, Parenthood, Sons of Anarchy and Bones.

Khleo Thomas began his career in music in 2003. His first track appeared on the soundtrack of Holes. Since then, he has released four mixtapes and two studio albums. He was also a guest performer on several tracks for artists such as Bow Wow and Soulja Boy. In 2011, he released a necklace line that he designed himself and is called “Love Wings.”
